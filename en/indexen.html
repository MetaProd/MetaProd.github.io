About

Discover me a little more.

  • (DATE)

    Discovery of the video

    Everything started from a meeting at high school time. A supervisor of my boarding school, he was producing videos for nightclubs, clips, etc... And this is when I saw him editing his videos that my passion for this job started.

  • (Date)

    First experience

    This is the "Vintage", formerly "Before du Palace", at Hyères, that gave me my first opportunity ! With a Canon 600D loaned by a friend, I made my very first after movie. It is from this moment that this job became an evidence for me.

  • (Date)

    Education

    Like a lot of people, I started to learn by myself, then I arrived at a point where I no longer progressed. So I passed a visual communication designer BTS, to improve my skills. This education now allow me to elaborate all kind of realisations, I any sector, with a preparation, a film shooting, a video compilation, and a professional rendering.

  • (Date)

    My vision of the video nowadays

    Knowing that our brain analyzes an image more than 2000 times faster than a text, video has taken a considerable place in the communication world. That's why a promotional video will have a bigger impact, on your customer base, than an article in a newspaper.
